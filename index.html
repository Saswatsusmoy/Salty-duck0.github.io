Cryptography using Discrete Mathematics

In our project , we developed a text encryption model using Python and concepts from Discrete Mathematics. By applying mathematical techniques like rearranging messages in unique ways, using matrices for added security, and employing number tricks, we aimed to create a strong encryption method. We also utilized modular arithmetic to make our encryption resistant to decoding attempts. Our focus was to ensure the program's efficiency and practicality.
